About Bindi and the CDDAP

Bindi Enterprises is proud to announce that this September will be the inaugural prize for excellence in Digital Media: The Central Desert Digital Arts Prize.

With a prize of $3000 for the winning work, this all-abilities art prize will showcase the artistic talent of those in and around Mparntwe/Alice Springs and invite national artists to share their talent with our community.

Bindi Enterprises is known for supporting creative excellence without limits. A Social Enterprise, Bindi has been providing services to people living with disability in the Central Desert since 1978.

KEY INFORMATION AND DATES

  • $3,000.00 non-acquisitive selected art prize
  • Entry forms available online only
  • Entries open: 1st June 2023
  • Entries close: 31st June 2023
  • Selected artists notified: 15 July 2023
  • Exhibition opens/winner announced: 23 September 2023
  • Exhibition closes: 8 October 2023
  • Exhibition dates: 24 September to 8 October at RAFT Artspace, Mparntwe/Alice Springs NT

CONDITIONS OF ENTRY

Eligibility

  • Australian residents of all abilities are eligible to enter
  • $25.00 entry fee for each artwork
  • Artists may submit up to 2 entries
  • The entry must be an original artwork completed within 12 months prior to the closing date
  • The selection process will be undertaken by a panel of arts professionals. The selection panels decisions are final.
  • Artists are responsible for transport of their artwork, delivery and collection

Artwork

  • Artwork must be digital ie: film/audio visual or photographic
  • Work must have been completed in the previous 12 months at the time of submitting the entry
  • The work must not exceed 1.5 x 1 metres
