CONDITIONS OF ENTRY

Eligibility

  • Australian residents of all abilities are eligible to enter
  • $25.00 entry fee for each artwork
  • Artists may submit up to 2 entries
  • The entry must be an original artwork completed within 12 months prior to the closing date
  • The selection process will be undertaken by a panel of arts professionals. The selection panels decisions are final.
  • Artists are responsible for transport of their artwork, delivery and collection

Artwork

  • Artwork must be digital ie: film/audio visual or photographic
  • Work must have been completed in the previous 12 months at the time of submitting the entry
  • The work must not exceed 1.5 x 1 metres

The Central Desert Digital Art Prize is generously funded by the Agnico Eagle Community Partnership Program.
